S.H.O.T. (2010)
HEAVY ROCK / METAL from BERLIN

Borned in Brazil, the band has been running since 2010 and in the beggining the four TERRON brothers (Lenny Terron - Vocals and Guitars | Henry Terron - Guitars | Mey Terron - Bass | Guiller Terron - Drums) left their houses to archive their dreams of playing music.
After a couple of years they finally found their base in Berlin where they decided to stay a little longer.
In 2014, two of the brothers left the band (Guiller and Mey) giving the spot to the Australian 'Geraint Morton' and the German 'Martin Aim' that joined permanently the band.
The band has released more than 4 EPs, 2 LPs and played in around 10 different countries.
Their sound is mixed up with heavy, metal and hardcore but their attitude is energetic and punk-rock.
In January 2018 the band has released their last LP entitled 'ANIMALS' and 3 MUSIC VIDEOS are already available on their youtube channel.


Looking forward to hear from you 
Henry Terron
Manager @ Backdoor Party & Green Valley Rock Festival
